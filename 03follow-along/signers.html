Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence, along with why they are well-known:

Thomas Jefferson
Main author of the Declaration, later became the third President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
Renowned polymath, diplomat, and one of the Founding Fathers of the United States.
John Adams
Advocate for independence, later became the second President of the United States.
John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress, famous for his large and bold signature on the Declaration.
Samuel Adams
Leader in the American Revolution and a key figure in organizing resistance against British policies.
Roger Sherman
Only person to sign all four great state papers of the United States: the Continental Association, the Declaration of Independence, the Articles of Confederation, and the Constitution.
Robert Morris
Financier of the American Revolution and a signer of the Declaration, Articles of Confederation, and the Constitution.

Each of these individuals played a significant role in shaping the early United States and their legacies continue to influence the nation today.
